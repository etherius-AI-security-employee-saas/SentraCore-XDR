EmailGuard Settings

Configure how the extension scans inbox content and handles auto-analysis for supported mail providers.

Protection Controls

Auto-Scan Emails Run EmailGuard when users open a message in Gmail or Outlook.
Local AI Engine Keep local decisioning active for fast phishing and scam detection even when cloud checks are slow.
Onboarding Completed Controls whether the welcome experience is shown after install.

Access & Mode

No license key required. EmailGuard protection is open for all users.

Support Notes

EmailGuard stores its scan preferences and history in Chrome local storage. It only activates on supported mail surfaces and is designed for customer-friendly deployment without exposing backend controls to end users.

Supported sites: mail.google.com, outlook.live.com, outlook.office.com
